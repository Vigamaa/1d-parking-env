About This Project

This is a 1D Parking Environment where a car moves along a straight line and needs to stop within a predefined parking zone. The car has a continuous state space (position and velocity) and three possible actions (accelerate backward, coast, or accelerate forward).

The environment follows Gymnasium standards and can be used for reinforcement learning experimentation. The Q-learning agent demonstrates how a computer can learn to solve this problem through trial and error.
